The People Behind the Vision

Meet the Team

Ascendant Ventures is powered by a diverse group of strategists, technologists, marketers, and advisors — each bringing deep expertise and a commitment to exceptional results.

Leadership

The Leadership Team

Abdelqader Al Kahloot
Co-Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Abdelqader Al Kahloot brings over 18 years of distinguished experience in banking, wealth management, and high-performance sales leadership. A Jordanian national based in Dubai, he has built an exceptional track record across HSBC and Mashreq Bank — where he currently serves as Head of Sales and Client Coverage for Mashreq Gold.

At Ascendant Ventures, Abdelqader drives business development and client relationships, leveraging his deep expertise in financial strategy, team leadership, and market expansion across the UAE and beyond. He holds a Bachelor's in Finance and Banking and is a CISI-certified professional in Wealth and Investment Management.

Business Development Wealth Management Strategic Sales Financial Services Team Leadership
Melvine Mathews
Co-Founder & Chief Operating Officer

Melvine Mathews is a highly versatile business leader with over two decades of experience spanning banking, technology consulting, data analytics, and digital innovation. Having built his career across Microland, Bangalore Labs, Binnie Black & Veatch, Standard Chartered Bank and Mashreq Bank in the UAE — holding senior roles including VP of Proposition, Portfolio & Products and Head of Priority & International Banking — Melvine brings rare depth across strategy, digital transformation, and analytical leadership.

At Ascendant Ventures, he channels this multi-disciplinary expertise into building a firm that sits at the intersection of consulting, technology, and marketing. Melvine holds an MBA in Finance from Lancaster University, UK, and a BE in Computer Science from Bombay University. He is also a practitioner of predictive analytics, AI/ML, blockchain, and modern web technologies.

Digital Innovation Business Analytics AI & ML Banking & Fintech Strategy
