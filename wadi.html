Revenue Engine

Every mandate type. Calculated live.

Wadi's calculation engine handles the full complexity of wealth management fee structures — from insurance retrocessions to carried interest — with no server round-trips.

15+ product types supported
Life insurance, savings plans, execution-only wealth management, advisory, discretionary, PE & alternatives, financing, custody, and more.
Booking type waterfall
Direct company bookings and introducer-routed deals handled separately. VAT applied before share splits. Introducer and RM commission auto-calculated.
No stored calculations
All figures computed on-the-fly from raw inputs stored in the database. Changes to inputs instantly update KPIs, pipeline tables, and revenue reports.
